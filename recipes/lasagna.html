lasagna

Description

This is a delicious vegatarian lasagna loaded with wonderful vegetables

Ingredients

  • one spaghetti squash
  • can of diced tomatoes
  • Italian seasoning
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozerella cheese
  • diced onion, diced red pepper, diced green pepper
  • sliced baby bella mushrooms
  1. cut spaghetti squash in half and bake for 45-50 minutes at 450
  2. While the spaghetti squash is cooking, sautee onions in Italian seasoning with salt and pepper
  3. After a few minutes when the onions are browned, throw in the peppers, another few minutes, the mushrooms, and lastly, the can of diced tomatoes, seasoning while you go
  4. let sauce simmer for 20 minutes
  5. when the spaghetti squash is done, scoop out all the insides keeping the shells intact
  6. In each shell, layer squash, sauce, ricotta chesse, mozerella cheese, squash, sauce, mozerella cheese
  7. bake for 20 minutes at 450

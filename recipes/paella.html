Lentil Stew

Description

This is a delicious lentil stew fully vegetarian and delicious

Ingredients

  • one cup of dried lentils, soaked for minimum 6 hours before cooking
  • diced tomato
  • seasoning
  • diced sweet potatoe
  • vegetable broth
  • diced onion, diced red pepper, diced green pepper
  • sliced baby bella mushrooms
  1. Sautee onions in seasoning with salt and pepper
  2. After a few minutes when the onions are browned, throw in the peppers, another few minutes, the mushrooms, and lastly, the diced tomato, seasoning while you go
  3. brown the lentils in the pot with the onions and everything
  4. throw in the sweet potatoes
  5. throw in the broth and bring to a simmer
  6. ready after about a half hour

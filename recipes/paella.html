Paella

Description

This is a delicious paella, substituting the typical meat options for chickpeas

Ingredients

  • one cup of white rice
  • can of chickpeas
  • seasoning
  • diced carrot
  • vegetable broth
  • diced onion, diced red pepper
  • peas
  1. Sautee onions in seasoning with salt and pepper, use lots of turmeric
  2. After a few minutes when the onions are browned, throw in the peppers, another few minutes, the mushrooms, and lastly, the diced tomato, seasoning while you go
  3. heat up the broth
  4. throw in the rice, toast it
  5. put the peas and carrots in the pan
  6. dumo the broth in and cook for about 15-20 minutes with the lid on
